Autonomus Boat

This is my first autonomus vheicle project! In this project I build an autonomus boat with cheep components to learn the basics of ardupilot and autonomus navigation.


Parts
2025/05/31

The first steps of the project was to figure out the minimal viable product. What was the bare minimium to make an atuonomus vheicle?

The most important thing for a boat is that it floats and does not take in water. I settled with a big plastic food container, because i knew that they had a water tight lid. If the boat where to roll over or loose signal it would not take in water. The compromise was however that I did not have very great hydrodynamics. But again, minimal viablie product.



Then I also needed a form of propulshun. I choose to have two motors with propellers in the water that could push and stear with diferential trust. These motors was cheep drone motors from Amazon, ment to fly drones and not boats. Therefore it was not an efficient combination, but they had more than enough power. The motors also came as a kit with speed controllers which saved me some money as well, and also enshured that they were compatible.



Next problem to takle was to keep the motors dry but the propeller in the water. I found hobby grade propellershafts on Amazon that i could attach to my motors. Then i could attach the axel sleeve (idk if that's the right term) to the hull with epoxy wich created a waterprof seal. Then the inside of the axle sleeve were filled with grease to prevent water from penetrating in to the hull.



In order to drive my motors i needed batteries. I settled with two 3cell 57,72Wh lipo batteries wich could drive my motors for quite some time. I also needed a way to controll my motors so i got a radio reciver that had the nessesary connections. And for a transmitter I choose a Raiomaster TX16S because it has a modular design that can transmit to almost any reciver.



With the components that I have gatherd at this point I could make a radio controld boat but not a autonomus boat. In order for it to be autonomus the boat needs to control it self based on feadback about it's sorundings. I needed a controll computer.

This type of comupter is often refered to as a flight computer because they are often used in drones and airplanes, but thay can esely be configured to control a car or a boat. The main thing that a flight computer has is gyro/accleroometer, gps, compas and telemetry radio. Several of these systems have reduncency wich is important if you whant your plane or drone to not fall out of the sky. In my project the stakes are not that high. If the gyro or gps should fail the boat will still float and I could manualy stear it back to shore. Althoug my project does not need or use all the feachers and i could maybye have done it with much cheeper electronics. A flight computer is still alot easyer to configure than programming an arduino to do somthing similar. Because there are already so much software already developed for these controllers it is much easyer to use what aready exixts than to reinvent the wheel. I also plan to use this flight controller on other projects that will use more feachers so it makes sensce to not have to upgrade down the line.

The flight computer is an Pixhawk 6c mini that is running Ardupilot and the ground software Im using is MissionPlaner.

Assebmely and testing
2025/06/01

Before I could make the boat autonomus I needed to make sure I could stear it as a regular RC boat. I connected one battery to two of the ESC's(electronic speed controller) which where connected to the motors. In order for the boat to turn I needed the motors to variy in speed when i steard left or right. With some youtube videos and tinkering I got it to work



However these drone motors where spinning way to fast for my boat, so most of the time I had to run them almost at the lowest speed. After installing the propeller axle on the "boat hull" I needed to test for leeks.



After I enshured no leaks I set out for the boat's maiden voyage. It was not fast nor streamlined but it worked.



Before I made it autonoums I whanted to improve the hydrodynamics, turning and make the propellers sumburge more. When testing, the boat leaned alot backwards which I whanted to improve. I put more weight in the front to balance it more. The result was that the boat bored it's way down in to the water. To fix that problem I made a nose of styrofoam to help the front of the boat come up from the water a bit more. It helped but the propellers where still to close to the water line, and at higher speeds the propellers would take in air and loose "connection" with the water. At low speeds it worked, so my crusing speed became roufly 1 m/s.



Making it autonomus
2025/06/01

In order for it to be autonomus i had to connect the brains to the eyes and arms. In this case the brains was the flight computer, eyes the gps and arms the motors. To continue the analogy you could say that the ears and voice of the boat weare the telemetry radio that constantly comuinicated with my computer. But more techinicaly I needed all of these systems to work together, this was not easy. The ESCs would not comunicate with the flight computer and the gps did not get a good signal. As it was my first time doing anything like this I felt quite out of my deapth. The details are not that important but after a lot of time looking at the ardupilot guide and trubleshooting for days, I finaly got it to work. It could drive autonomus and follow waypoints. But but the summer last year, when I did this, was comming to an end and I felt finished with the project. Eventhoug my time ran out that time now I whant to come back to the project and to do a v2 of the boat. Everyting worked good exeped the hull design. So that was my first thing to takle.
